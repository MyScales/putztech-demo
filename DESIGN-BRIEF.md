# Putztech GmbH - Premium Redesign Brief

## Research Findings (Feb 7, 2026)

### Award-Winning Sites Analyzed:
- **OH Architecture** (Awwwards SOTD 7.42) - Horizontal scroll, page transitions, draggable gallery
- **GKC Architecture** (Awwwards SOTD 7.29) - 2-color palette, strong project pages
- **Brightstone** (Top Construction 2026) - Minimalist luxury, "Live Brighter" storytelling
- **Turner Construction** - Full-width hero video, clean typography

### Key Principles for "WOW" Effect:

1. **LIGHT AESTHETIC** — White/cream backgrounds, not dark mode tech-bro
2. **BOLD TYPOGRAPHY** — Oversized headlines (80px+), confident
3. **GENEROUS WHITESPACE** — Let content breathe, luxury = space
4. **SMOOTH ANIMATIONS** — GSAP scroll-triggered, subtle reveals
5. **FULLSCREEN IMAGERY** — Dominating visuals (or honest placeholders)
6. **STORYTELLING COPY** — "We craft..." not "Our services include..."
7. **REAL TESTIMONIALS** — Or none at all
8. **HORIZONTAL ELEMENTS** — Break vertical monotony
9. **PROCESS VISUALIZATION** — Numbered steps, clear journey
10. **MINIMAL COLOR PALETTE** — 1-2 colors max + neutrals
